A Django and Android based feedback collection and analysis system

New

Critique is a Django based application that communicates with an Android app to collect feedback for various courses offered in an institute.

Critique generates useful visualisations and uses natural language processing to understand the collected feedback and give insights to instructors about the functioning of the course. Critique is licensed under the GNU General Public License v3.0 and is open source and free to use.
